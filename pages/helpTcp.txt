R turns the LED red (255,0,0)
G turns the LED green (0,255,0)
B turns the LED blue (0,0,255)
O turns the LED off (0,0,0)
#,#,#U turns the LED to a Specific color (#,#,#)
#D delays the execution by # of milliseconds

ex...
R1000DG1000DB1000DO
This will turn the LED red then green then blue then
off while waiting one second before each change.
